HOW TO PLAY

Select a unit by clicking it on the board.

Move — click Move, then click a blue tile to move there.

Attack — click Attack, then click a red-highlighted enemy.

Skill — choose a skill from the submenu, then select a target.

Wait — skip this unit's remaining actions for the turn.

End Turn — finish all player actions and let enemies act.


Units:

Bear (B) — Tough brawler. Moves forward or sideways. Knockback skill.

Wolf (W) — Nimble striker. L-shaped movement. Howl buffs attack.

Crow (C) — Flying scout. Flies anywhere within range 5. Long-range dive.


Terrain: Forest tiles provide cover (+1 defense). Water and Mountains are impassable.


Defeat all enemies to win the stage.
